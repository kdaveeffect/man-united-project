How I got here

Thats right, videogames! I was at a friends place, and he was playing the cover off of this Fifa 2008 game. The demo. I picked up the stick (controller) and played..and was hooked! He would pick Barcelona, I picked Manchester United (I had heard of them before) and hours and hours of our young adulthood were vanishing deep into the night...or day..or whatever time it was! So the hours and hours invested in this game, made me wonder about the "beautiful game" in real life. With his help and the prior investment I made Manchester United my squad!
